When to Choose BJT vs FET

BJT (Bipolar Junction Transistor)

Best for audio amplification, radio circuits, LED drivers, and low-frequency switching. Current-controlled with high gain.

FET / MOSFET

Best for high-speed switching, motor control, power supplies, and high-frequency circuits. Voltage-controlled with low gate draw.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to match polarity?

Yes. NPN must replace NPN, and N-Channel must replace N-Channel. They are not interchangeable.

2. Can I use a higher voltage rating?

Yes. Using a part with a higher Vds or Vceo is safer and often recommended. Never use a lower rating.
